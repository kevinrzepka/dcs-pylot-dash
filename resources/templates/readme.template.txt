# Installation of %app_title% %app_version%

1. Go to your DCS Scripts folder, which is likely located at C:\Users\<username>\Saved Games\DCS\Scripts\
2. Append the content of add-to-Export.lua to your existing Export.lua in your DCS Scripts folder
3. Copy %output_script_name% to your DCS Scripts folder
4. Place the %html_file_name% anywhere you like, e.g., on your Desktop

# Usage of %app_title% %app_version%

1. Open %html_file_name% in your browser
2. Start a mission in DCS and observe the data being shown in your browser
